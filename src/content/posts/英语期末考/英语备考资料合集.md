# 第一篇：写作范文

## 范文一：The Belt and Road Initiative and University Students

> As China plays a bigger role in world affairs, what new skills or qualities should university students develop to take part in projects like the Belt and Road Initiative? Explain why these are important for your own growth.

**As China plays an increasingly important role on the global stage, university students need to cultivate new skills and qualities to actively participate in international projects like the Belt and Road Initiative.**
随着中国在全球舞台上扮演越来越重要的角色，大学生需要培养新的技能和素质，以积极参与"一带一路"等国际项目。

**In my opinion, cross-cultural communication skills and foreign language proficiency are the two most essential qualities.**
在我看来，跨文化沟通能力和外语水平是最重要的两项素质。

**First, cross-cultural communication skills enable us to understand and respect different cultural backgrounds, which is crucial when working with people from diverse countries.**
首先，跨文化沟通能力使我们能够理解和尊重不同的文化背景，这在与来自不同国家的人合作时至关重要。

**Second, mastering at least one foreign language, especially English, allows us to express our ideas clearly and collaborate effectively with international partners.**
其次，掌握至少一门外语，尤其是英语，能够让我们清晰地表达自己的想法，并与国际伙伴有效合作。

**These skills are important for my personal growth because they broaden my horizons and help me become a global citizen.**
这些技能对我的个人成长很重要，因为它们能拓宽我的视野，帮助我成为一个具有全球视野的公民。

**Moreover, they enhance my competitiveness in the job market and prepare me for future challenges in an interconnected world.**
此外，它们还能提升我在就业市场上的竞争力，让我为这个互联互通世界的未来挑战做好准备。

## 范文二：Protecting Cultural Heritage in My Hometown

> Cultural heritage conservation is important. As a college student, what can you do to help protect the cultural heritage in your hometown? Share two specific ideas and explain how they might work.

**Cultural heritage is a precious treasure that carries the history and wisdom of our ancestors, and protecting it is everyone's responsibility.**
文化遗产是承载着祖先历史和智慧的宝贵财富，保护它是每个人的责任。

**As a college student, I can contribute to cultural heritage conservation in my hometown through two specific approaches.**
作为一名大学生，我可以通过两种具体的方式来为家乡的文化遗产保护做出贡献。

**First, I would use social media platforms to create short videos and posts introducing the cultural heritage sites in my hometown to a wider audience.**
首先，我会利用社交媒体平台制作短视频和帖子，向更广泛的受众介绍家乡的文化遗产。

**By doing so, more people, especially young people, would become aware of these cultural treasures and develop a sense of pride and responsibility to protect them.**
这样一来，更多人，尤其是年轻人，会了解这些文化瑰宝，并产生保护它们的自豪感和责任感。

**Second, I would volunteer at local museums or cultural sites during holidays, helping with guided tours and basic maintenance work.**
其次，我会在假期期间到当地的博物馆或文化遗址做志愿者，协助导游讲解和基础维护工作。

**This hands-on involvement would not only support the preservation efforts but also deepen my own understanding and appreciation of our cultural heritage.**
这种亲身参与不仅能支持保护工作，还能加深我自己对文化遗产的理解和珍视。

## 范文三：Should Young People Focus on Studies Instead of Public Service?

> Some people think young people should focus on their studies instead of spending time on public service. Do you agree or disagree? Give at least two reasons to support your opinion.

**Some people argue that young people should concentrate solely on their academic studies rather than spending time on public service, but I respectfully disagree with this view.**
有些人认为年轻人应该专注于学业，而不是花时间从事公共服务，但我对此观点不敢苟同。

**I believe that participating in public service is not a waste of time but a valuable supplement to academic learning.**
我认为参与公共服务并不是浪费时间，而是对学术学习的有益补充。

**First, public service helps students develop a sense of social responsibility and empathy, which cannot be learned from textbooks alone.**
首先，公共服务有助于培养学生的社会责任感和同理心，这些是仅从课本上学不到的。

**For example, volunteering at a nursing home teaches us to care for the elderly and appreciate the importance of companionship.**
例如，在养老院做志愿者教会我们关爱老人，并懂得陪伴的重要性。

**Second, engaging in public service allows students to apply what they have learned in real-world situations, enhancing their practical skills.**
其次，参与公共服务能让学生将所学知识应用于实际情境中，提升他们的实践能力。

**In conclusion, I believe that studies and public service are not mutually exclusive, and a proper balance between the two can lead to well-rounded personal development.**
总之，我认为学业和公共服务并非相互排斥，在二者之间找到适当的平衡可以实现更全面的个人发展。

## 范文四：Working Alone or in a Team?

> Some people prefer to work alone, while others prefer to work in a team. Which do you think is better for college students, and why? Give at least two reasons to support your opinion.

**When it comes to the choice between working alone and working in a team, I believe that teamwork is more beneficial for college students.**
关于独自工作和团队合作之间的选择，我认为团队合作对大学生更为有益。

**My opinion is based on the following two reasons.**
我的观点基于以下两个理由。

**First, teamwork cultivates communication and cooperation skills, which are essential in both academic life and future careers.**
首先，团队合作能够培养沟通与协作能力，这在学术生活和未来职业中都是必不可少的。

**Through group discussions and collaborative projects, we learn to listen to others, express our own ideas, and resolve conflicts constructively.**
通过小组讨论和协作项目，我们学会倾听他人、表达自己的想法，并以建设性的方式解决冲突。

**Second, working in a team allows us to learn from each other and make up for our own weaknesses.**
其次，团队合作让我们能够互相学习、取长补短。

**When we tackle a complex task together, each member can contribute their unique strengths, making the final outcome far better than what any individual could achieve alone.**
当我们共同完成一项复杂任务时，每个成员都能贡献自己独特的优势，使最终成果远超个人单打独斗所能达到的水平。

**Therefore, I strongly recommend that college students embrace teamwork as a valuable learning experience.**
因此，我强烈建议大学生将团队合作视为一种宝贵的学习经历。

## 第一部分（共17题）

### 1. The government's early \_\_\_\_\_\_ in the housing market helped prevent a sharp rise in prices.

A. interruption　　B. intervention　　C. interference　　D. interaction

> **答案：B**

**📖 译文**：政府对住房市场的早期**干预**有助于防止房价的急剧上涨。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. interruption**：打断、中断（多指打断别人讲话或某动作）
- **B. intervention** ✅：（中性偏正面）干预、介入，常用于政府/官方主动调控
- **C. interference**：（贬义）干涉、妨碍，多指不受欢迎的插手
- **D. interaction**：互动、相互作用

**💡 记忆要点**：政府"干预" → intervention（中性）；别人"瞎掺和" → interference（贬义）。

---

### 2. The \_\_\_\_\_\_ of Chinese cuisine, from spicy Sichuan dishes to delicate Cantonese dishes, reflects the country's rich regional cultures.

A. diversion　　B. density　　C. dimension　　D. diversity

> **答案：D**

**📖 译文**：中国菜的**多样性**，从辛辣的川菜到精致的粤菜，反映了这个国家丰富的地方文化。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. diversion**：转移、消遣
- **B. density**：密度
- **C. dimension**：维度、尺寸
- **D. diversity** ✅：多样性、多元化

**💡 记忆要点**：diverse（多样的）→ diversity（多样性）。常考短语 **cultural diversity**（文化多样性）、**biological diversity**（生物多样性）。

---

### 3. To some degree, the success of the project \_\_\_\_\_\_ on teamwork, though individual effort also matters.

A. insists　　B. consists　　C. depends　　D. decides

> **答案：C**

**📖 译文**：在某种程度上，项目的成功**取决于**团队合作，尽管个人努力也很重要。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. insist on**：坚持（主张）
- **B. consist of**：由……组成；**consist in**：在于
- **C. depend on** ✅：取决于、依赖于
- **D. decide**：决定（及物动词，不与 on 搭配）

**💡 记忆要点**：固定搭配 **depend on / rely on**（依靠）。注意 decide 后不加介词 on，直接接宾语。

---

### 4. Peking Opera, a treasure of Chinese culture, is \_\_\_\_\_\_ by a unique combination of singing, dancing, martial arts, and elaborate costumes.

A. characterized　　B. distinguished　　C. classified　　D. identified

> **答案：A**

**📖 译文**：京剧，作为中国文化的瑰宝，**以**唱、念、做、打和精美服饰的独特结合**为特征**。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. be characterized by** ✅：以……为特征
- **B. be distinguished from**：与……区别开来（搭配 from）
- **C. be classified as**：被归类为
- **D. be identified as**：被认定为

**💡 记忆要点**：**be characterized by + 特征**（核心搭配）。区分介词：by（特征）、from（区别）、as（归类/认定）。

---

### 5. In 2024, "Spring Festival" was officially \_\_\_\_\_\_ on the UNESCO Representative List of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity.

A. described　　B. inscribed　　C. prescribed　　D. subscribed

> **答案：B**

**📖 译文**：2024年，"春节"被正式**列入**联合国教科文组织人类非物质文化遗产代表作名录。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. describe**：描述
- **B. inscribe** ✅：铭刻、（正式）列入名录
- **C. prescribe**：开处方、规定
- **D. subscribe to**：订阅、赞同

**💡 记忆要点**：词根 **-scribe**（写）。inscribe = in（向内）+ scribe（写）= 刻入、载入。固定表达：**be inscribed on the list**（被列入名录）。

### 6. With the rapid development of 5G technology, China has emerged as a global leader in telecommunication \_\_\_\_\_\_.

A. invasion　　B. inspiration　　C. invention　　D. innovation

> **答案：D**

**📖 译文**：随着5G技术的快速发展，中国已成为电信**创新**领域的全球领导者。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. invasion**：入侵
- **B. inspiration**：灵感
- **C. invention**：发明（具体产物）
- **D. innovation** ✅：创新（过程或理念，更现代）

**💡 记忆要点**：**invention** = 从无到有的发明；**innovation** = 现有基础上的创新改进，与科技、商业搭配频率更高。

### 7. What an extraordinary performance the young pianist gave last night that everyone stood up and \_\_\_\_\_\_ for five minutes.

A. acclaimed　　B. appreciated　　C. applauded　　D. applied

> **答案：C**

**📖 译文**：昨晚那位年轻钢琴家的演奏是多么不凡，以至于所有人都起立**鼓掌**长达五分钟。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. acclaim**：（口头）称赞、欢呼
- **B. appreciate**：欣赏、感激（不指鼓掌动作）
- **C. applaud** ✅：鼓掌、喝彩（鼓掌的标准动词）
- **D. apply**：申请、应用

**💡 记忆要点**：stood up and **applauded** = 起立鼓掌。名词形式 **applause**（掌声）。

### 8. Encouraging youth participation is not just about preparing the next generation of leaders — it is about creating a more inclusive and \_\_\_\_\_\_ society today.

A. responsive　　B. responsible　　C. respective　　D. response

> **答案：A**

**📖 译文**：鼓励青年参与不仅是培养下一代领导者——更是为了打造一个更包容、更**积极回应**的社会。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. responsive** ✅：（对需求）积极回应的、反应灵敏的
- **B. responsible**：负责任的
- **C. respective**：各自的
- **D. response**：（名词）回应

**💡 记忆要点**：与 inclusive（包容的）并列，应选形容词且语义相关。**a responsive society** 指能"听到民声、回应民意"的社会。

### 9. The United Nations' \_\_\_\_\_\_ on Climate Change brings together world leaders to discuss solutions for global warming.

A. gathering　　B. conference　　C. assembly　　D. press

> **答案：B**

**📖 译文**：联合国气候变化**大会**汇集了世界各国领导人来讨论应对全球变暖的方案。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. gathering**：聚会（非正式）
- **B. conference** ✅：（专题）大会、会议
- **C. assembly**：议会、集会（如 UN General Assembly 联大）
- **D. press conference**：新闻发布会（press 单独不行）

**💡 记忆要点**：**conference on + 主题**（关于……的大会）为固定表达，常见如 climate conference、academic conference。

---

### 10. It is the diversity of human civilizations \_\_\_\_\_\_ drives the progress of humankind.

A. which　　B. what　　C. that　　D. who

> **答案：C**

**📖 译文**：正是人类文明的多样性**才**推动了人类的进步。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- 这是 **It is/was ... that ...** 强调句型
- 无论强调主语、宾语、状语，连接词都用 **that**
- 强调人时也可用 who，但 that 永远适用

**💡 记忆要点**：强调句口诀——**It is + 被强调成分 + that + 其余部分**。判断：去掉 It is...that 后句子完整即为强调句。

---

### 11. \_\_\_\_\_\_ improving material well-being, we should also enrich our spiritual life.

A. While　　B. When　　C. Although　　D. However

> **答案：A**

**📖 译文**：**在**改善物质生活的**同时**，我们也应该丰富精神生活。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. While** ✅：在……的同时（可接动名词，表伴随）
- **B. When**：当……时（多接句子或时间点）
- **C. Although**：虽然（表让步，语义不符）
- **D. However**：然而（副词，不能引导短语）

**💡 记忆要点**：**While + doing**（在做……的同时，做……）是经典写作句型。

### 12. The professor's lecture was so complex that it was completely \_\_\_\_\_\_ the understanding of most first-year students.

A. above　　B. beyond　　C. over　　D. under

> **答案：B**

**📖 译文**：教授的讲座非常复杂，完全**超出了**大多数一年级学生的理解能力。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. above**：在……之上（位置）
- **B. beyond** ✅：超出（能力/范围）
- **C. over**：在……上方/超过（数量）
- **D. under**：在……之下

**💡 记忆要点**：固定搭配 **beyond one's understanding/reach/control/imagination**（超出某人的理解/触及/控制/想象）。

### 13. China has gained helpful experience in \_\_\_\_\_\_ the relationship between urban development and the protection of these sites. **Which answer can't be used in the blank.**

A. addressing　　B. handling　　C. approaching　　D. facing

> **答案：D**（选不能用的）

**📖 译文**：中国在**处理**城市发展与遗址保护的关系方面积累了有益的经验。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. addressing**：处理、应对（问题）✓
- **B. handling**：处理、应付 ✓
- **C. approaching**：着手处理 ✓
- **D. facing** ❌：面对（面临问题，但不含"处理"含义，与 experience in 搭配不当）

**💡 记忆要点**：本题考"处理"近义辨析。address / handle / approach / deal with = 处理；face = 面对（不等于处理）。

### 14. The idea of a community of shared future for mankind has been \_\_\_\_\_\_ by the Chinese leader on various diplomatic occasions since 2012.

A. exposed　　B. expanded　　C. explored　　D. expounded

> **答案：D**

**📖 译文**：自 2012 年以来，中国领导人在多个外交场合**阐述**了人类命运共同体的理念。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. expose**：暴露、揭露
- **B. expand**：扩展、扩大
- **C. explore**：探索、探究
- **D. expound** ✅：详细阐述（思想/理论）

**💡 记忆要点**：**expound** 专门用于"阐述思想、理论、观点"，比 explain 更正式。常见搭配：expound a theory / an idea。

### 15. The committee suggested \_\_\_\_\_\_ a new approach to solving the traffic problem in the city center.

A. adopting　　B. adapting　　C. adjusting　　D. adoring

> **答案：A**

**📖 译文**：委员会建议**采纳**一种新方法来解决市中心的交通问题。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. adopt** ✅：采纳、采用（方法/方案/孩子）
- **B. adapt**：适应、改编
- **C. adjust**：调整
- **D. adore**：崇拜、喜爱

**💡 记忆要点**：经典易混词——**adopt**（采纳）、**adapt**（适应）、**adept**（熟练的）。adopt a new approach / measure / policy。

### 16. In recent years, many cultural scholars are concerned about the gradual disappearance of some traditional Chinese folk arts \_\_\_\_\_\_ lack of young inheritors.

A. as to　　B. owe to　　C. due to　　D. thanks to

> **答案：C**

**📖 译文**：近年来，许多文化学者担忧一些中国传统民间艺术**由于**缺乏年轻传承人而逐渐消失。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. as to**：至于、关于（不表原因）
- **B. owe to**：把……归功于（用法不对，应为 owing to）
- **C. due to** ✅：由于、因为（中性偏负面原因）
- **D. thanks to**：多亏（褒义，语义不符）

**💡 记忆要点**：表"由于"的近义短语——**due to / owing to / because of**（中性）；**thanks to**（褒义，"多亏"）。

### 17. The vision of a community of shared future for mankind, once \_\_\_\_\_\_, has been widely recognized in the world.

A. putting forth　　B. put forth　　C. was put forth　　D. is putting forth

> **答案：B**

**📖 译文**：人类命运共同体的理念一经**提出**，便在世界上得到了广泛认同。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- 主语 The vision 与 put forth（提出）是被动关系
- 状语从句省略：**once (it was) put forth** → 保留过去分词

**💡 记忆要点**：**once / when / if + 过去分词**（一旦/当/如果被……）。例：Once finished, the project will... 。

## 第二部分（共17题）

### 1. The nation's rapid economic growth and social progress in recent decades have presented both challenges and opportunities for the \_\_\_\_\_\_ of its heritage resources.

A. reservation　　B. conservation　　C. preservation　　D. observation

> **答案：B**

**📖 译文**：近几十年来国家的经济快速发展与社会进步，给遗产资源的**保护**带来了挑战和机遇。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. reservation**：预订；保留地
- **B. conservation** ✅：（资源/环境/文化的）保护
- **C. preservation**：保存（强调维持原状不变质）
- **D. observation**：观察

**💡 记忆要点**：**conservation** 用于自然资源、文化遗产的"保护性利用"；**preservation** 强调"原样保存"。heritage 多搭配 conservation。

### 2. One person's mood and outlook can spread within a team, so a \_\_\_\_\_\_ team member could negatively influence the way the whole group views its goals.

A. optimistic　　B. realistic　　C. positive　　D. pessimistic

> **答案：D**

**📖 译文**：一个人的情绪和观念会在团队中传播，因此一位**悲观的**队员可能会负面地影响整个团队对目标的看法。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. optimistic**：乐观的
- **B. realistic**：现实的
- **C. positive**：积极的
- **D. pessimistic** ✅：悲观的（与 negatively 搭配）

**💡 记忆要点**：根据 negatively influence（负面影响）反推，应为消极态度。**optimistic ↔ pessimistic**（乐观↔悲观）。

### 3. Thanks to recent efforts, a great number of \_\_\_\_\_\_ protected sites have been effectively conserved with their settings and surroundings remarkably improved.

A. prioritized　　B. preferred　　C. primary　　D. previous

> **答案：A**

**📖 译文**：得益于近期的努力，大量**被优先**保护的遗址得到了有效保护，其环境和周边显著改善。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **B. coalition**：（政治）联盟 ✓
- **C. alliance**：同盟、联盟 ✓
- **D. alien** ❌：外星人、外国人（与同盟无关）

**💡 记忆要点**：**military alliance**（军事同盟）= 经典搭配。alien 易与 ally 混淆，注意区分：al**ien** = 异乡人；**ally** = 盟友。

### 15. The prime minister will meet his French \_\_\_\_\_\_ next week to discuss trade agreements between the two nations.

A. opposite　　B. colleague　　C. equal　　D. counterpart

> **答案：D**

**📖 译文**：首相下周将会见他的法国**同级官员**，讨论两国之间的贸易协议。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. opposite**：相反的（形容词常用）
- **B. colleague**：同事（同一机构内）
- **C. equal**：同等的人（不限身份）
- **D. counterpart** ✅：对应方、（不同机构中）职位相对应的人

**💡 记忆要点**：**counterpart** 专用于"不同国家/组织中职位相对应的人"。如：The American president and his Chinese **counterpart**.

### 16. After winning the election, the new president worked hard to \_\_\_\_\_\_ his power and gain wider support from the public.

A. consolidate　　B. console　　C. consist　　D. conform

> **答案：A**

**📖 译文**：赢得选举后，新总统努力**巩固**自己的权力，并争取更广泛的公众支持。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. consolidate** ✅：巩固、加强
- **B. console**：安慰
- **C. consist**：组成（不及物）
- **D. conform**：遵从、符合

**💡 记忆要点**：**consolidate one's power / position / influence**（巩固权力/地位/影响力）。与 strengthen 同义。

### 17. Unlike the classical musicians, the young composer preferred to create modern works that felt fresh rather than \_\_\_\_\_\_.

A. ancient　　B. antique　　C. old　　D. aged

> **答案：A**

**📖 译文**：与古典音乐家不同，这位年轻作曲家更喜欢创作那些感觉新鲜而非**陈旧**的现代作品。

**🔍 选项辨析**：

- **A. ancient** ✅：古老的、陈旧的（与 fresh / modern 形成对比，正式书面）
- **B. antique**：古董的（多指具体物品）
- **C. old**：老的（口语化，对比力度弱）
- **D. aged**：上了年纪的（多指人或酒类陈化）

**💡 记忆要点**：与 **modern / fresh** 强烈对比，正式语境选 **ancient**（古老 vs 现代）。antique 强调"古董价值"，old 太普通。
